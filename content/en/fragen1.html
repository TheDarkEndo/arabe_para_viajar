Initial questions
How do I get started?

    It is very simple: On the cover of your CD you will find the name of a file. Just open the corresponding file with any Web-browser (Internet Explorer, Netscape, Firefox, Safari etc.)

What adjustments do I need to make on my browser?

    • Please enable JavaScript.
    • Please enable cookies.
    • Should you be using the Internet Explorer please choose the option "very low" from the security settings menu.

    You can make the necessary adjustments by opening the following control panels from your browser:
    Internet Explorer: Tools/Internet options
    Firefox/Mozilla/Netscape: File/options
    Safari: Safari/settings

What's the best way to start?

    It is best to start memorising the words onscreen. Do listen to them at least a couple of times. Afterwards you should print the vocabulary onto index cards. It is best to print the two languages separtely to front and back (Use the option: "vocabulary/ index cards". Proceed by clicking on the printer icon). With the help of the index cards, try to memorise the words and their translation. Once you feel secure you may test your achievements on your computer (Choose "Vocabluary/ test" from the menu)

I cannot view the lessons!

    Please enable JavaScript or switch to a different Web-browser.

Some windows will not open!

    Please disable any pop-up blocking software.

Do I have to be online?

    No, you do not need to be online to work with the language course. If your browser wants to connect to the internet please choose the option "offline" from your browser menu.
