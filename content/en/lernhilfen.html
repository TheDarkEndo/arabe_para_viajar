learning aids
This programme offers a variety of learning aids that should make it easier for you to memorise all the words.

Repeat 7:

    A quick repetition will automatically start whenever you have got seven words wrong during a test.
    A status bar is included to indicate how long you have spent on the repetition. It takes one minute for the bar to turn completely green.

Break after 30 minutes:

    A break symbol will appear once you have spent 30 minutes on a test. You should take a five minutes' break until the lights go back to green.

Time indicators for studying single words:

    The programme contains a time indicator which is meant to give you an idea how long you have spent on memorising the word on diplay. Every five seconds a tiny dot will appear below the word.

Display of quotes:

    Quotes will appear on various occasions to give you tips that should make studying easier for you.

Effective repetitions:

    During the tests you will be asked a word until you get it right at least once. You will only be asked those words more than once that you did not already know. For all the other words one correct entry is sufficient.
